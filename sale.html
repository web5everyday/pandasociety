How to mint?

The easiest way is to install a MetaMask extension in your Chrome browser and link your Ethereum wallet to it. Ensure you have enough ETH in your wallet to cover the cost of a NFT plus the associated transaction (gas) fees. Approve the transaction on MetaMask and you are all set. The Karafurus will be available to mint on our official website.
